- Most AI video generation companies have NOT adopted the llms.txt standard
- Companies with llms.txt tend to be platform/API-first (OpenAI, Google, HeyGen, Higgsfield)
- Chinese-origin tools (Kling, Seedance, Hailuo, Vidu, Wan) universally lack llms.txt
- Creative/consumer-focused tools (Pika, Runway, Midjourney) also lack llms.txt
- Avatar-focused tools (HeyGen, Synthesia, D-ID) show mixed adoption (only HeyGen)
